Detailed Description

multiplex to DAB transmission frames

block multiplexes the FIBs of the FIC and all subchannels of the MCI to a transmission frame according to ETSI EN 300 401 the number of FIBs per CIF and the number of CIFs per transmission frame depends on the transmission mode the maximum number of supported sub-channels is 7
